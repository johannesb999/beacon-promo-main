How it Works

Reasons why Beacon

  1. 01

    Always a safe path

    Every hold maps the calmest route to the nearest safe spot.

  2. 02

    You're never alone

    Nearby Beacons light up and move toward you in real time.

  3. 03

    Have a clear next step

    A direction arrow always points you toward what's next.

Beacon turns the people and places around you into a living safety net. One hold of a button quietly calls for help — and your neighbourhood lights up to guide you home.

How to Use

  1. 1.

    See your surroundings

    A live map shows your location and the Beacons reachable around you.

  2. 2.

    Hold to activate help

    The button arms quietly. A route to the best Spot appears, with alternatives and a direction arrow.

  3. 3.

    Beacons light up

    Nearby helpers and Safe Spots are activated and signal that they're on the way.

  4. 4.

    You get help

    You recognize your helper by their blinking flashlight; your phones confirm the connection. Stop the alarm at any time with “I feel safe”.

Join Beacon and help
